\subsection{Standard Notation} 
The toric notation used in this article will be the standard one. We will not
reintroduce the full definition of a toric variety each time it appears. Instead,
for a toric variety \(X\), we always regard it as equipped with its acting
algebraic torus
\[
T\cong(\mathbb C^*)^n
\]
and with the usual dual lattices
\[
N=\operatorname{Hom}(\mathbb C^*,T),
\qquad
M=\operatorname{Hom}(T,\mathbb C^*).
\]
The lattice \(N\) is the lattice of one-parameter subgroups, while \(M\) is the
character lattice. They satisfy
\[
M=\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb Z}(N,\mathbb Z).
\]
We denote their realifications by
\[
N_{\mathbb R}=N\otimes_{\mathbb Z}\mathbb R,
\qquad
M_{\mathbb R}=M\otimes_{\mathbb Z}\mathbb R.
\]

In this article, our main theroem is the an equivalence of stable $\infty$-categoriy
\begin{align*}
    \kappa:\operatorname{Perf}_T(X_\Sigma) \xrightarrow{\sim} \operatorname{Sh}_{cc}(M_{\mathbb{R}},\Lambda_{\Sigma})
\end{align*}
